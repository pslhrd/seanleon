FROM THE MIND OF SEAN LEON
Sean Leon is a visionary. His talents have earned him international praise and acclaim including collaborations with artists such as Brandy, Pharell Williams, Daniel Cesar, Justin Bieber and Kanye West.A distinctive voice, Sean’s perspective challenges widespread conceptions of what it means to be an artist aware of their humanity and fragility. Finding inspiration within a pool of genres, experiences of love, fatherhood, and loss; his creative approach, much like his artistic spirit, is fearless and without boundaries. Since independently releasing, “Sean Leon (The Death of)”, Leon set his sights on broadening his international presence. In 2019, he embarked on an international tour performing, creative directing, and songwriting alongside Daniel Caesar. While abroad, Leon quickly caught the eye of international icons, including Kanye West, who came to feature his placements on his 2019, Grammy award winning studio album, "Jesus is King". His latest venture, PUPIL, is a production and cultural organization that creates success stories through ethical development, design and distribution.
