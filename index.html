WHAT IS REALITY ?
01010100 01001111
The fine line between reality and digital existence is in disarray as humans have gripped onto online forms of communicating and creating. This human relationship to technology has been further heightened by the global pandemic. It leads us to wonder, what is real when we’ve spent more than a whole year living online? What have we learned? What are we losing? And is technology curing our loneliness, or feeding it?
IS THERE HUMANITY WITHIN THE SIMULATION?
Does digital equate to artificial, or are digital experiences, emotions, and adventures serving as vehicles when no other transportation is available? Are computed, and digital emotions less meaningful than traditional ones? How could they be if they resonate deep within us?
